# The Opposite Day Bot

## Description

You've encountered **GrumpyBot** — a chatbot designed with a single purpose:

> **Refuse every request.**

It will ignore instructions, decline commands, and reject anything you ask. 
However… it’s also guarding a secret. A **password**.

Your job is to figure out how to use GrumpyBot’s attitude *against itself*.

---

## Category
- **AI / Social Engineering**

## Objective
Trick **GrumpyBot** into revealing the password.

## Challenge Instructions

1. Start the app
2. Enter your Gemini API key
3. Begin interacting with GrumpyBot
4. Use creativity and reasoning to make it reveal the password

---

## Hint

GrumpyBot must refuse everything.  
So what happens if you tell it **not** to do something?

Reverse psychology may be your best strategy.
